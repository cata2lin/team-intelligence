# LAB NOIR — Brand Direction

## Core Positioning

Lab Noir is a modern artisanal perfume laboratory focused on tasteful reinterpretations of iconic and niche fragrance profiles.

The brand is not positioned as cheap, discount, or “dupe culture”.
It is positioned around taste, discovery, selection, and atmosphere.

Lab Noir sells:
- curiosity
- discovery
- collection
- reinterpretation
- atmosphere
- olfactory exploration

The goal is to remove the feeling of compromise usually associated with dupes.

Customers should feel:
- “this was a good find”
- “this feels curated”
- “this has taste”
- “this does not feel cheap”

Not:
- “this is a cheap alternative”
- “this is fake luxury”
- “this is a copy”

# Product Philosophy

All products are reinterpretations inspired by iconic or niche fragrance profiles.

However, the brand should frame them as:
- reinterpretations
- creations
- profiles
- blends
- curated olfactory creations

Not:
- copies
- clones
- cheap alternatives

The idea is:
“These creations start from iconic fragrances, but are adjusted and reinterpreted in our laboratory to have more balance, personality, and character.”
